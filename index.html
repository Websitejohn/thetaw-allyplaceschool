About Us

Premier ABA Therapy

At Westside ABA, we are committed to delivering exceptional Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy to children with autism and related developmental disorders. Our dedicated team of professionals employs the latest evidence-based practices to create personalized treatment plans that cater to each child's individual needs, fostering their growth and development in a supportive and nurturing environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

ABA (Applied Behavior Analysis) therapy is a type of behavioral therapy that helps individuals improve their behaviors and skills through positive reinforcement and structured interventions.

The duration of ABA therapy varies depending on individual needs and goals. Therapy can range from a few hours a week to several hours a day, often over an extended period.

Many insurance plans cover ABA therapy, but coverage can vary. It is best to check with your insurance provider to understand your benefits and any requirements for coverage.
