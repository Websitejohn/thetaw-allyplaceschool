The Taw-Ally Place School

Welcome to The Taw-Ally Place School, where learning meets creativity and curiosity is encouraged!

Our school is dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. With our experienced faculty and innovative teaching methods, we strive to inspire a love for learning in every child.

Located in the heart of our community, our school is surrounded by natural beauty and provides a serene setting for education. We believe in the importance of holistic development, fostering not only academic excellence but also character building and personal growth.

At The Taw-Ally Place School, we embrace diversity and celebrate individuality. We encourage our students to explore their passions, express their creativity, and discover their unique talents.

Come join us on this exciting journey of discovery and exploration. Together, let's empower our children to reach their full potential and become lifelong learners.


Mission Statement

"To provide a nuturing and innovative school that is passionate about creating a safe and stimulating learning environment which encourages creativity, curiosity and critical thinking."


Join us as we lay the foundtion for a future of endless possibilities.

Our Environment

Active Learning, Expert Teachers & Safe Environment

We provide a dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to participate actively in their education. Our expert teachers are dedicated to fostering a love of learning in each student, ensuring they reach their full potential.

Our classrooms are designed to be safe and nurturing spaces where students can explore new ideas, ask questions, and develop their skills. We believe that a supportive environment is key to academic success and personal growth.

Our curriculum is tailored to meet the needs of all learners, incorporating modern teaching techniques and a variety of educational resources to make learning engaging and effective.
